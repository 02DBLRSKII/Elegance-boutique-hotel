About Us

Built with intent, run with care

Our Story

A different kind of stay near the airport

Elegance Boutique Hotel was built in Tedstonville, Germiston, to answer a simple question: why should a stay near the airport feel like an afterthought? The result is a property designed with the same care as a city-centre boutique hotel — split-face stone, glass balustrades, a glazed rooftop pergola — set against landscaped gardens and mature date palms.

Every detail, from the tiered water features at the entrance to the lighting design in each suite, was chosen to make guests slow down the moment they arrive, whether they're here for one night or several.

Our Mission

Comfort that doesn't need to shout

We built Elegance Boutique Hotel around a straightforward idea: exceptional design and genuine hospitality should be available for a single night, not reserved for a special occasion.

Considered Design

Every suite is finished with intent — from brass fixtures to lighting design — rather than standardised hotel fittings.

Genuine Hospitality

A small, attentive team who know the property and the area, on hand without ever being intrusive.

Quiet Reliability

Consistent, well-maintained rooms and grounds — the kind of dependable comfort late arrivals and early flights need.

Our History

How the property came together

Phase 01

Site & design

The Tedstonville site was selected for its position between Germiston's city centre and O.R. Tambo, and the contemporary architectural concept was developed around light, stone, and glass.

Phase 02

Construction

The main building took shape — split-face stone columns, glass balustrades, and a glazed rooftop pergola structure — alongside the landscaped gardens and water features.

Phase 03

Interiors & suites

Each of the seven suites was finished individually, with its own lighting design and furnishings, rather than a single repeated room template.

Phase 04

Pool terrace & indoor pool

The outdoor pool terrace and separate indoor pool were completed, rounding out the property's amenities.

Today

Open for guests

Elegance Boutique Hotel now welcomes guests year-round, from overnight travellers to longer Gauteng stays.
